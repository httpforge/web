Frequently Asked Questions

Is HttpForge free to use?

Yes. HttpForge is open source and free to use for personal and commercial projects. There are no usage limits, subscription tiers, or hidden fees.

Where are my API credentials stored?

All credentials and environment variables are encrypted and stored locally on your device. HttpForge does not transmit your secrets to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Which platforms are supported?

HttpForge currently offers a native Windows desktop application and a Visual Studio Code extension. Support for additional platforms is on our roadmap.

How do I report a bug?

Open an issue on the GitHub repository with steps to reproduce, expected behavior, actual behavior, and your operating system version. Include screenshots or request logs when possible.

Can I contribute to HttpForge?

Absolutely. We welcome contributions of all kinds — code, documentation, design, and testing. Visit our Contributors page to get started.

Contact
