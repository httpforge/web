Privacy Policy

Open-source API client for designing, testing, and managing HTTP requests.

Privacy First

We do not store your requests, responses, API keys, or personal information on our servers.

No analytics. No tracking. No user profiling.

Your data stays on your device and remains fully under your control.

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Overview

HttpForge is designed with privacy as a foundational principle. The API client operates primarily on your local device, and we minimize the collection of personal data wherever possible.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to HttpForge servers:

  • API credentials, tokens, and environment variables
  • Request collections and history
  • Application preferences and settings

Local data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption. You are responsible for securing your device and backing up your data.

Website

The httpforge.com website does not use analytics, tracking pixels, or user profiling. We do not collect personal data through the website or sell data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

When you send API requests through HttpForge, those requests are transmitted directly to the target servers you specify. HttpForge does not intercept, log, or proxy your API traffic. Third-party services you interact with are governed by their own privacy policies.

GitHub

If you interact with our GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions), your activity is subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Data Retention

Local application data persists until you delete it or uninstall the application. We do not retain user data on our servers.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Contact us at httpforge@outlook.com to exercise these rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

Privacy inquiries: httpforge@outlook.com
